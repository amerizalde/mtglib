## Rules Text
Choose up to five P worth of modes. You may choose the same mode more than once.
P - Each player sacrifices a creature of their choice.
PP - Draw a card for each creature that died under your control this turn.
PPP - Each opponent loses X life, where X is the number of creature cards in your graveyard.
